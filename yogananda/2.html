"Simple living, high thinking."

Simple living refers to living a life that is not focused on materialistic things, such as possessions or wealth. Instead, it emphasizes the importance of living a life that is focused on spiritual growth and personal development. It means living a life that is free from unnecessary complications and distractions.
High thinking refers to the process of developing one's mind and intellect. This involves focusing on personal growth and development, as well as spiritual growth. It means striving to improve oneself intellectually and spiritually, and to become a better person overall.
